What to do after an accident

Stay calm. Follow these steps.

Call Emergency (000) Call Police Assistance (131 444)

Steps:

  • Check everyone is safe
  • Move to a safe spot if possible
  • Take photos of damage, number plates, and surroundings
  • Exchange details
  • Do NOT admit fault
  • Contact your insurer
Designed for Australian drivers 🇦🇺
